About Our System

"The 'Grow Guardian Greenhouse' system is an environmental monitoring solution designed for greenhouse management. It enables real-time monitoring of crucial parameters such as humidity, temperature, light intensity, and moisture. Additionally, it offers control over various actuators including fans, heaters, water pumps, and bulbs. This comprehensive system ensures optimal growing conditions for plants within the greenhouse, promoting efficient and healthy growth."
